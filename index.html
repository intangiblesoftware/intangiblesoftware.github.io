About Intangible Software

It's been good folks! I started Intangible Software back in 2012 as a way to sell my software as I started to get into iOS software development. Since that time I've had several apps in the app store focusing mainly on lacrosse stats-keeping. Lately, my real job has kept me from keeping up with supporting that software. Since I can't, in good conscience, keep selling software I can't maintain, I've removed them from the app stores.

I hope you enjoyed and got good use out of my software: Men's Lacrosse Stats, Women's Lacrosse Stats, and Time of Possession. I enjoyed building and using them myself for years. You may still contact me using the form below if you have any questions, comments or concerns.
